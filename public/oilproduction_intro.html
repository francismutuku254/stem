Oil Production Rig Project Overview

Key Components of the Rig

Base Platform

The large black and yellow plates forming the foundation where the whole structure is mounted. Provides stability.

Motor & Power Hub

The white programmable brick (with ports) and the medium motor (cylindrical, grey/white) which drive the drilling motion.

Vertical Support Frame

The blue and black beams standing upright, connecting the base to the top section. Acts like the “drill tower.”

Drilling Arm / Boom

The horizontal beam assembly at the top (blue and yellow bricks) which extends outward and holds the moving drill mechanism.

Gear & Axle Mechanism (Drill Bit)

The axle and gears extending downward, transferring rotation from the motor to simulate drilling.
