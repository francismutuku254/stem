Acebott Smart Car Project with Camera

Assembly Steps

Introduction

In the previous tutorial we built the Smart Car. In this tutorial, we'll integrate a camera using the QD002 expansion pack.
Disconnecting Wires

Unplug the wires from the car shield.

Separating the Upper and Lower Assemblies

Undo the 6 screws on the long pillars. Return the screws in their packaging. Seperate the upper and lower acrylic assemblies.
Removing the Ultrasonic Sensor

Unplug the ultrasonic module. Undo the small servo screw on servo arm. Remove the ultrasonic module. Also remove the servo arm from the bracket.

Removing the LED modules and Servo

Unplug the modules. Undo the M3 nuts on the module screws, and the M2 nuts on the servo.

Installing the Servo Arm on Camera Bracket

Install the servo arm on the bracket. Ensure the notch is on the upper left with respect to the servo hole.

Assembing Camera Frame

Mount the side plates on the top camera plate using the flathead M3 screws and M3 nuts. Ensure the servo arm is on the inner side of the bracket. Also, make sure the servo hole is aligned with the hole on the opposing bracket plate.

Mounting the camera in its Frame

Insert the camera module with the port facing up.Secure with the M2 screws and nuts.

Mounting the Servo

Place the servo in the servo plate and secure with M2 screws and nuts. The servo shaft should face up.

Mounting Servo Assembly

Secure servo bracket side plates onto base plate using flathead M3 and M3 nuts. Mount servo bracket assembly to lower acrylic board using short M3 screws and M3 nuts.
Installing the Camera Assembly

Partially install the long flathead M3 screw in is hole. Insert the isolation column in the hole and hold it using the screw. Slide in the camera assembly then insert the screw fully. Ensure it is aligned with the servo. Fasten using the lock nut.

Mounting Ultrasonic Module

Mount ultrasonic module in its bracket and secure with the screws and nuts. Connect wires as described below:

  1. Red wire - V
  2. Blue wire - T
  3. White wire - E
  4. Black wire - G
Assembling the Car

Position the ultrasonic assembly. Slide on the upper assembly. Secure the boards with short M3 screws.

Wiring

Connect the 4-pin cable to the camera module as follows:

  1. White wire - 14
  2. Blue wire - 13
  3. Black wire - GND
  4. Red wire - 5V
Next wire up the com ponents as listed here. Make sure red wire always corresponds to V pin:
  1. Servo - 26
  2. Camera - UART
  3. Buzzer - 33
  4. IR receiver - 4
  5. Line tracker - LINETRACK
  6. Ultrasonic - ULTRASONIC
  7. Front Left motor - Top left motor port
  8. Front Right motor - Top right motor port
  9. Rear Left motor - bottom left motor port
  10. Rear Right motor - bottom right motor port
