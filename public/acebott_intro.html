Acebott Smart Car Project Overview

Assembly Steps

Introduction

This is the Acebott QD001 Smart Car. We'll assemble it in this tutorial.

Line Sensor

Peel the protective paper from lower acrylicc board and install line sensor. Secure with M3 screws and screws.

Long Pillars

Install long pillars on lower acrylic board. Secure with short M3 screws.

Motors

Secure motor with bracket and long M2 screws and nuts. Mount the motor assembly to lower acrylic board. Secure with short M3.

Controller and Car Shield

Peel protective paper from upper acrylic board. Install short pillars and secure with short M3. Mount and secure controller board with short M3 screws.

LED, Buzzer, IR

Connect 3-pin cable to the modules; red wire to V pin. Mount and secure the modules with short M3 screws and nuts.

Servo

Insert the servo in its mounting hole. Secure it with M2 screws and nuts.

Battery Holder

Align the battery holder holes with the holes on the board. Secure it with flathead M3 screws and M3 nuts.

Ultrasonic Sensor

Peel off protective paper from bracket. Mount sensor on bracket with pins facing away from servo mount. Secure with M2 screws and nuts.

Joining Assemblies

Join the upper and lower assemblies. Secure along pillars with short M3 screws.

Wheels

Insert wheel coupling on motor shaft. Insert the wheel and secure with long self-tapping screws.

Wiring

Wire up the components as listed here. Make sure red wire always corresponds to V pin.

  1. LED module - 2
  2. LED module - 12
  3. Buzzer - 33
  4. IR receiver - 4
  5. Servo - 26
  6. Line tracker - LINETRACK
  7. Ultrasonic - ULTRASONIC
  8. Front Left motor - Top left motor port
  9. Front Right motor - Top right motor port
  10. Rear Left motor - bottom left motor port
  11. Rear Right motor - bottom right motor port

Batteries

Slide the switch on the battery holder to the OFF position. Insert 2 18650 cells in the battery holder. Verify the polarity. Install CR2025 coin cell in the IR remote.

Programming

Copy and upload the source code. Feel free to modify it here and there.
